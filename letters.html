Monotype developed many of the most widely used typeface designs, including Times New Roman, Gill Sans, Arial, Bembo and Albertus.Arial contains 51 styles and family package options. It is based on the Monotype Grotesque type face. All versions of Microscoft Windows after 3.1, as well as Micosoft programs, Apple's macOS, and many PostScript 3 printers use arial.
